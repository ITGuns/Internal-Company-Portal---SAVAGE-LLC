# Product

## Register

product

## Users

MyDeskii is used by SAVAGE LLC employees, managers, operations staff, payroll reviewers, and admins during normal work hours. Users move between task tracking, daily logs, payroll review, announcements, chat, files, and employee approval workflows while focused on getting work recorded and reviewed accurately.

## Product Purpose

MyDeskii is an internal operating portal for daily work coordination. It should help employees see what needs attention, track work, submit daily logs, communicate with the team, review files, and support payroll accuracy. For managers and admins, it should make approvals, task oversight, time review, and operations controls clear without exposing sensitive details.

## Brand Personality

Practical, precise, and calm. The interface should feel direct and dependable, with enough polish to show care but no decorative styling that slows repeated use.

## Anti-references

- Marketing landing pages used as dashboard layouts.
- Heavy dark themes that make forms and tables harder to read during daytime work.
- Purple or blue AI-style gradients.
- Nested cards, glassmorphism, glowing buttons, and oversized hero sections.
- Modal-first workflows when inline panels or side panels would keep context clearer.

## Design Principles

- Put daily work first: prioritize the next useful action over decorative layout.
- Keep context visible: users should understand status, ownership, due dates, and required follow-up at a glance.
- Use restraint as polish: rely on spacing, alignment, typography, and state clarity instead of visual noise.
- Preserve trust: authentication, approval, payroll, and admin screens must make risk and permission boundaries obvious.
- Design for repetition: common actions should feel fast, predictable, and accessible.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast for text and controls. Preserve keyboard navigation, visible focus states, responsive layouts, reduced-motion behavior, semantic landmarks, and clear empty, loading, error, disabled, and success states.
